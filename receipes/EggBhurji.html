Egg Bhurji

Description

Egg Bhurji is a popular Indian dish made by scrambling eggs with onions, tomatoes, and a blend of spices.

It is often served with roti, bread, or rice as a flavorful, quick meal.

Ingredients

Serves 2

  • Eggs - 4 large eggs
  • Onions - 1 medium-sized onion, finely chopped
  • Tomatoes - 1 medium-sized tomato, finely chopped
  • Ginger - 1 teaspoon, finely grated or minced
  • Garlic - 1 teaspoon, minceds
  • Fresh coriander leaves - 2 tablespoons, chopped
  • Turmeric powder - 1/4 teaspoon
  • Red chili powder - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Garam masala - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Cumin seeds - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Salt - to taste
  • Black pepper - 1/4 teaspoon
  • Oil or butter - 2 tablespoons for cooking/li>

Steps

  1. Heat oil/butter: In a pan, heat 2 tablespoons of oil or butter over medium heat. Add 1/2 teaspoon of cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Sauté onions and spices: Add 1 finely chopped onion, 1-2 chopped green chilies, and 1 teaspoon each of minced ginger and garlic. Sauté until the onions turn golden brown. Then, add 1/4 teaspoon of turmeric powder, 1/2 teaspoon of red chili powder, and salt to taste. Stir well.
  3. Cook tomatoes: Add 1 chopped tomato to the pan and cook until it softens and becomes pulpy, about 3-4 minutes.
  4. Scramble the eggs: Crack 4 eggs into the pan, and scramble them into the onion-tomato mixture. Cook on low to medium heat, stirring continuously, until the eggs are cooked through but still soft and fluffy.
  5. Garnish and serve: Sprinkle 1/2 teaspoon of garam masala and add 2 tablespoons of chopped fresh coriander leaves. Give it a final mix, and your Egg Bhurji is ready to serve with roti, bread, or rice.
